About Us

Welcome to RamSai IAS

Sri Ram Muppalla, a double Post- Graduate (or having acquired)in Mathematics and Computer Science Engineering, has put over 27+ years of Academic Experience. He founded Comprehensive Learning Institute for Creativity and Knowledge ( CLiCK) in 2006, to nurture thousands of students to get into reputed MBA colleges across the Nation. CLiCK also trained 100s of aspirants to become Probationary Officers through IBPS and Group-2 Positions in various departments of Union Govt through SSC- CGLE. With this kind of rich experience, he incorporated RamSai IAS Academy in 2018 to offer the Result Oriented Training Program for limited aspirants carefully handpicked through Admission & Oral Tests.

RamSai IAS is unique in the sense that it offers the classroom as well as Online Program only for Limited Aspirants with the Best Faculty from NEWDELHI.
